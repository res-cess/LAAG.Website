MASBATE CITY

BUNTOD REEF MARINE SANCTUARY AND SANDBAR

A 251-hectare Marine Protected Area (MPA) located off the waters of Masbate City, it is best known for its abundance of helmet shells,spowdery white 1-hectare sandbar as well as a healthy numberof mangroves at the far end of the sandbar. The sanctuary serves as a barrier island protecting the main land from wave attack, storm surge, tidal range,and sediment transport.

  • Part of island hopping package, swimming, snorkeling, scuba diving, sunset watching, beach combing, and water sport activities
